# Explain Result AI Assistant - User Guide

## What is it?

The **Explain Result AI Assistant** helps farmers understand their crop disease analysis reports by answering questions in simple language. It's powered by Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash AI and supports Tamil, Hindi, and English.

---

## How to Use

### Step 1: Complete a Crop Analysis

1. Open the **AI Plant Doctor** page
2. Upload a crop leaf image or use your camera
3. Wait for the AI to analyze the image
4. Scroll down to see your complete analysis report

### Step 2: Find the Explain Result Assistant

After your analysis is complete, scroll down to find the **"Explain Result"** section (or **"முடிவை விளக்கு"** in Tamil, **"परिणाम समझें"** in Hindi).

It looks like this:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🔍 Explain Result                       │
│ Ask about any term or recommendation... │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ 🌾 AI: Hi! I can explain any word...   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ [Quick questions as buttons]            │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ [Type your question here...] [Ask]      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 3: Ask Questions

You have two ways to ask:

#### Option A: Click a Quick Question Button

The assistant shows common questions like:
- **English**: "What is 'Rice Blast'?", "What does confidence score mean?", etc.
- **Tamil**: "நம்பிக்கை மதிப்பெண் என்றால் என்ன?", etc.
- **Hindi**: "कॉन्फिडेंस स्कोर क्या होता है?", etc.

Just click any button to get an instant answer.

#### Option B: Type Your Own Question

Type any question in the text box and press Enter or click "Ask". Examples:

**English**:
- "What is confidence score?"
- "How do I use neem oil?"
- "Why is my crop diseased?"
- "What dosage should I use?"

**Tamil**:
- "நம்பிக்கை மதிப்பெண் என்றால் என்ன?"
- "வேப்ப எண்ணெய் எப்படி பயன்படுத்துவது?"
- "உரம் எவ்வளவு போட வேண்டும்?"

**Hindi**:
- "कॉन्फिडेंस स्कोर क्या होता है?"
- "नीम तेल कैसे उपयोग करें?"
- "उर्वरक की मात्रा कितनी डालें?"

### Step 4: Read the Answer

The AI will respond in 2-3 seconds with:
- ✅ **Short, clear answer** (150-200 words)
- ✅ **Simple language** (no complex jargon)
- ✅ **Relevant to YOUR crop analysis** (not generic advice)
- ✅ **In your selected language** (Tamil/Hindi/English)

## What You Can Ask

### ✅ Good Questions:

1. **Definitions**: "What is [term]?"
   - "What is Rice Blast?"
   - "What is organic solution?"
   - "What does severity mean?"

2. **How-to**: "How do I...?"
   - "How do I apply neem oil?"
   - "How do I prevent this disease?"
   - "How much fertilizer should I use?"

3. **Why**: "Why...?"
   - "Why is my crop diseased?"
   - "Why do I need organic solutions?"
   - "Why is humidity mentioned?"

4. **Dosage/Amount**: "What amount...?"
   - "What dosage is safe?"
   - "How much water do I need?"
   - "What fertilizer amount?"

### ❌ Questions It Won't Answer:

- Questions about OTHER crops not in your report
- General farming questions unrelated to your analysis
- Questions about weather in other regions
- Personal medical advice
- Business/market predictions

**The assistant only explains YOUR current analysis report.**

## Language Support

### Automatic Language Detection

The assistant automatically responds in your app's current language:
- If your app is in **English** → Answers in English
- If your app is in **Tamil** → Answers in Tamil
- If your app is in **Hindi** → Answers in Hindi

### Changing Language

To change the language:
1. Go to **Settings** (or **அமைப்புகள்** / **सेटिंग्स**)
2. Select your preferred language
3. Return to your analysis report
4. The assistant will now respond in the new language

## Tips for Best Results

### ✅ DO:

1. **Ask specific questions** about YOUR analysis
   - Good: "What is the cure method mentioned?"
   - Bad: "Tell me about all tomato diseases"

2. **Ask one thing at a time**
   - Good: "How do I use neem oil?"
   - Bad: "How do I use neem oil, what is organic farming, and when should I harvest?"

3. **Use simple language**
   - Good: "What is confidence score?"
   - Bad: "Could you please elaborate on the statistical significance and methodological approach to confidence interval estimation?"

4. **Ask for clarification if needed**
   - "Can you explain that in simpler words?"
   - "What does that mean?"

### ❌ DON'T:

1. Don't ask about other crops not in your report
2. Don't ask multiple questions in one message
3. Don't expect full treatment plans (already in your report)
4. Don't ask unrelated farming questions

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Assistant not appearing

**Solution**: Complete a crop analysis first. The assistant only appears after you get analysis results.

### Problem: Answers in wrong language

**Solution**: Check your app language settings. The assistant follows your app's current language.

### Problem: "Could not get answer" error

**Solution**: 
- Check your internet connection
- Try asking a simpler question
- Refresh the page and try again
- If problem persists, contact support

### Problem: Answer is too technical

**Solution**: Ask: "Can you explain that in simpler words?" or "What does [term] mean?"

### Problem: Answer not related to my crop

**Solution**: Make sure your question is about the terms/recommendations in YOUR analysis report.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is the Explain Result Assistant free?**  
A: Yes, it's included with your AgriAid.AI account at no extra cost.

**Q: How accurate are the answers?**  
A: The assistant explains YOUR specific analysis results. The accuracy depends on the quality of your crop image and the AI's analysis.

**Q: Can I save the answers?**  
A: Currently, answers are not saved automatically. Take screenshots if you want to keep them.

**Q: How many questions can I ask?**  
A: You can ask as many questions as you need about your current analysis.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: No, you need an internet connection to use the Explain Result Assistant.

**Q: Can I use voice input?**  
A: Currently, only text input is supported. Voice input may be added in future updates.

**Q: What if I speak a different language?**  
A: Currently supported: Tamil, Hindi, English. More languages may be added based on user demand.
